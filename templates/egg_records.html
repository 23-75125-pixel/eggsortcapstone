Sorting Classification Standards

EGGSor+ IoT weight measurements adhere strictly to localized commercial standards: Small (>50g), Medium (50g to 56g), Large (57g to 63g), Extra Large (64g to 70g), and Jumbo (>70g). Handlers can calibrate automatic vision scaling directly from the control nodes on site.
